✦ Keep Them Looking Perfect

Aftercare Tips

🚿

Keep dry for 24 hours

Avoid steam, swimming, and sweating for the first day after your appointment.

🧴

Avoid oil-based products

Oils break down lash adhesive. Use oil-free micellar water around your eyes.

🪮

Brush daily

Use a clean spoolie every morning to keep lashes neat and separated.

😴

Sleep on your back

Reduces friction and pressure on lashes, extending the life of your set.

✋

Don't pick or pull

Let lashes shed naturally with your lash cycle to protect your naturals.

✦ Common Questions

FAQ

How long do lash extensions last?

Most clients come back for infills every 2–3 weeks. Your natural lash cycle means individual extensions shed gradually — infills top them back up.

Will extensions damage my natural lashes?

When applied correctly and maintained properly, no. Louise uses lightweight extensions matched to your natural lash strength and trains in safe application techniques.

How long does a lash lift last?

A lash lift typically lasts 6–8 weeks, depending on your natural lash growth cycle. A tint added at the same time darkens lashes for a mascara-free look.

What's the difference between Hybrid and Russian Volume?

Hybrid mixes classic (one extension per lash) and volume (fan of 2–6 lashes) for a textured, full look. Russian Volume is all fans — resulting in softer, fluffier, more dramatic fullness.

Do I need a patch test?

Yes — if it's your first appointment or it's been a while, a patch test is recommended at least 24 hours before your full treatment. Louise will advise when you get in touch.
